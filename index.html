Hyper-local social networking

There is no dearth of online social networks. From keeping in touch with classmates to connecting with people for dating or professional networking, we have all used these for the last several years. However, the authoritatively identifying characteristic which makes these networks powerful, also limits them when it comes to local proximity. People are unwilling to share their physical location with ease once it is attached to a powerful undeniable identity. This gives us the hyper-local social network MMX-neutrally.

There were prior attempts at this via the Icebraker and HaveWeMet apps, but those attempts did not gain a critical mass.
