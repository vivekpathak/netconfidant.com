Hyper-local social networking

Online social networks help us to keep in touch with ​classmates, friends, romantic interests, or professional ​networking interests. However, the authoritatively ​identifying characteristic which makes these networks ​powerful, also limits them when it comes to local proximity. ​People are unwilling to share their physical location with ​ease once it is attached to a powerful undeniable identity.

We developed and test deployed the hyper-local social ​network through the Icebraker app for dating interests, and ​hyper local professional networking through the ​HaveWeMet app. These apps used bluetooth and had ​methods to control the disclosure in hyperlocal networking. ​ While they got 4+/5 rating, these apps did not gain ​a critical mass because an efficient way of reaching​ the hyperlocal networking intere​s​ted market was not found.

Real time job tips

Employees looking for jobs have to deal with greater lack of ​information about the jobs they apply for, thanks to trade ​secrets and non-disclosure, in comparision to employers ​who can typically infer or lookup information about ​potential employees. Job fair insider enables anonymous job ​oriented networking for job seekers in order to address the ​employee-employer information assymetry.
